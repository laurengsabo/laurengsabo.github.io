About Me

I’m a Bioinformatics MS student at Georgia Tech with a background in Computer Science and Data Science.

I am also a Co-Founder and Chief of Product Design at Quuri, a platform designed to connect academics and researchers, and participated in the Fusen Accelerator Program in Summer 2024.

As an undergraduate, I built computational pipelines for genome assembly and functional annotation in African cichlids, establishing a strong foundation in large-scale genomics workflows and software engineering. Today, my graduate research focuses on probabilistic modeling for genotype inference and genomic mapping, with a passion for integrating computing, biology, and data visualization to drive interdisciplinary impact.

In my free time, I can be found on the Chattahoochee River as the Head Coach of the Novice Women's Squad of the GT Rowing Team. I enjoy teaching the fundamentals of rowing and encouraging a positive training environment on a team with historic success.
